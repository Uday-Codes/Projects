Learning to be a developer

Hello there! I'm Uday Deshmukh, a passionate and dedicated web developer with a lifelong fascination with coding. Growing up, I was always drawn to the creative and problem-solving aspects of programming, and I've been hooked ever since I wrote my first line of code. With a strong foundation in computer science and a keen eye for design, I've honed my skills in a range of programming languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.
